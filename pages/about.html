Who Are We

Xpert Rentals believe in Partnerships – Partnerships bring respect and mutual success

Xpert Rentals is the Full-Service Property & Real Estate Asset Management division of Burgeon Group. We are currently providing professional property management services for Calgary, Edmonton and surrounding Counties. The management team has a combined professional property management experience and expertise of over 17 years in Alberta.

Xpert Rental’s professionals have extensive experience and in-depth knowledge in large and small size real estate portfolio management with cutting edge tenant placement marketing and operating cost saving strategies for a sustainable management of your real estate investments.

Xpert Rentals stives on strong management principles – Plan, Organize and Execute with utmost transparency & commitment.
